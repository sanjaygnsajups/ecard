About The Modern Living

TML is a Venture of Ion Exchange India Ltd.

Ion Exchange India Ltd, a pioneer of water treatment in India with a legacy spanning over five decades is today a premier company in water and environment management with a global presence. Formed in 1964, as a subsidiary of the Permutit Company of UK, Ion Exchange became a wholly Indian company in 1985 when Permutit divested their holding.

A leading solutions provider, one of very few companies worldwide with complete range of solutions for Water, Wastewater Treatment, Solid Waste Management and Waste to Energy. Ion Exchange tech-driven products and services cater to industries, institutions, municipalities, homes and communities both urban and rural.

With sales, production and service footprints across the globe, Ion Exchange serves its markets with a sustained focus on customer satisfaction, technological innovation and dedicated service.
